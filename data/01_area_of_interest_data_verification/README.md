# Area of Interest Data Verification

This folder contains the initial and verified images of the area of interest. These images are used to validate the region and ensure the accuracy of the data collection process.

## Contents
- **Initial Clearing Images**: Images showing the area before any significant changes.
- **Rapid Expansion Images**: Images documenting rapid changes in the area over time.

## Purpose
The data in this folder is used for:
- Ground truthing and verification.
- Establishing a baseline for comparison with future data.
